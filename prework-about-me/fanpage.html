Bio

Hall & Oates are an American pop rock dou formed in 1970 in Philadelphia. Daryl Hall is generally the lead vocalist; John Oates primarily plays electric guitar and provides backing vocals. The two write most of the songs they perform, separately or in collaboration.


Hall and Oates have sold an estimated 40 million records. They have had six No.1 hits on the Billboard Hot 100.
